Meet The Foxes...?

Messenger Fox is somewhat the "Mayor" of Kitsune no Shakai. She delivers news rain or shine, she will get the job done! Also loves matcha :)

Little Sister Fox is Messenger Fox's younger sister. She is weirdly obsessed with boxes and is the most mischievous fox (as of now).

Otaku Fox is a gamer and a collector who stays indoors most of the time. Will only go out to buy merch or if lured by food.

School Girl Fox is still schooling and already hustling! You're lucky if you spot her relaxing, it's really rare.

Patisserie Fox has a sweet tooth that turned her hobby into her career! Will make desserts for you if she thinks you're trustworthy.

Farmer Fox may seem grumpy all the time, but it's actually very friendly! Don't be shy to say hi!
