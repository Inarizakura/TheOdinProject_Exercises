Tamagoyaki (卵焼き)

Tamagoyaki is a Japanese omelette made by rolling together thin layers of seasoned egg in a frying pan.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons dashi soup
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl. Add dashi, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. Mix well.
  2. Heat oil in a rectangular frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour a thin layer of egg mixture into the pan. Cook until set but still slightly runny.
  4. Roll the cooked egg from one end of the pan to the other.
  5. Push the rolled egg to the other end of the pan. Add more egg mixture to the pan.
  6. Lift the rolled egg and let the new mixture flow underneath. Cook until set but still slightly runny.
  7. Roll the cooked egg from one end of the pan to the other.
  8. Repeat steps 5-7 until all the egg mixture is used.
  9. Remove the rolled egg from the pan and let cool slightly. Slice and serve.
